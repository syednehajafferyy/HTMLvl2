Microsoft Teams

Whether you’re connecting with your community for an upcoming activity or working with teammates on a project, Microsoft Teams helps bring people together so that they can get things done. It’s the only app that has communities, events, chats, channels, meetings, storage, tasks, and calendars in one place—so you can easily connect and manage access to information. Get your community, family, friends, or work mates together to accomplish tasks, share ideas, and make plans. Join audio and video calls in a secure setting, collaborate in documents, and store files and photos with built-in cloud storage. You can do it all in Microsoft Teams.

Microsoft Onenote

Microsoft OneNote is a digital notebook that provides a single place for all your notes and information. Whether you’re brainstorming ideas, taking lecture notes, or keeping track of to-do lists, OneNote helps you organize your thoughts and collaborate with others. With its flexible canvas, you can type, write, draw, and clip web pages, making it easy to capture and share information. Plus, with built-in cloud storage, your notes are always accessible from any device.

Microsoft Excel

Microsoft Excel is a powerful spreadsheet application that allows you to organize, analyze, and visualize data. Whether you’re creating budgets, tracking expenses, or performing complex calculations, Excel provides a wide range of tools and features to help you work efficiently. With its intuitive interface, you can easily create charts, graphs, and pivot tables to gain insights from your data. Plus, with built-in collaboration features, you can share your spreadsheets with others and work together in real-time.

Microsoft Word

Microsoft Word is a word processing application that allows you to create, edit, and format documents. Whether you’re writing reports, creating resumes, or drafting letters, Word provides a wide range of tools and features to help you produce professional-quality documents. With its intuitive interface, you can easily add text, images, tables, and other elements to your documents. Plus, with built-in collaboration features, you can share your documents with others and work together in real-time.

Microsoft Outlook

Microsoft Outlook is an email and personal information management application that helps you stay organized and connected. Whether you’re managing your email, calendar, contacts, or tasks, Outlook provides a comprehensive set of tools to help you stay on top of your schedule and communications. With its intuitive interface, you can easily send and receive emails, schedule appointments, and manage your to-do lists. Plus, with built-in collaboration features, you can share your calendar and tasks with others and work together in real-time.

Microsoft Access

Microsoft Access is a database management application that allows you to create, manage, and analyze data. Whether you’re building a simple contact list or a complex inventory system, Access provides a wide range of tools and features to help you work efficiently. With its intuitive interface, you can easily create tables, forms, and reports to organize and present your data. Plus, with built-in collaboration features, you can share your databases with others and work together in real-time.
